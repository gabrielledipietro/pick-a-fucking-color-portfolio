Bone Folders + Blue Gloves is an ABSURD shopping experience that explores data collection, OCD and consumer culture in a new way. Germs are all around us, and most we can’t even see. For some this ignorance is welcome, but for others it’s a nightmare. As a germaphobe, I’m constantly aware of everything I touch or eat in fear of something being “infectious”. This thesis allows one to see the world of the overlooked (GERMS!) through the eyes of a germaphobe. The elements and things of spaces and places that exist around us but aren’t necessarily seen will be made tangible through various mediums. My research aims to uncover the curiosities of being a germaphobe through new different ways of germs cataloguing.
